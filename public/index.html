https://help@heroku.com

There are two steps for any custom domain. First the domain has to be registered for the correct application. I can see that the domains www.scripthammer.com and scripthammer.com are both listed under the stark-spire-40922 application. Remove them either individually with
heroku domains:remove or clear them all with
heroku domains:clear


        heroku domains:remove -a stark-spire-40922

        heroku domains:clear -a stark-spire-40922

//Then you can do
        heroku domains:add www.scripthammer.com -a fast-retreat-54148

        heroku domains:add scripthammer.com -a fast-retreat-54148

        heroku domains -a fast-retreat-54148

https://DNSSEC_@_Heroku.com

The second step is to make sure DNS records are correct. Luckily, in this situation they won't have changed since you already had it working for stark-spire-40922 so you don't have to do anything here, but you can always double check by running heroku domains -a fast-retreat-54148 and it will show the records you'll need to have which you can check your DNS provider to see if they are correct.
